CAPGEMINI NEW JOINER ONBOARDING GUIDE
Welcome to the team!

1. DAY 1 CHECKLIST
Your first day sets the foundation for a successful tenure at Capgemini.
- Report to the designated office location by 9:00 AM local time.
- Meet your assigned HR representative in the lobby to receive your welcome kit.
- Attend the mandatory 'Welcome to Capgemini' induction session from 10:00 AM to 12:30 PM.
- Complete all pending HR documentation and submit original educational certificates for verification.
- Receive your temporary 7-day access badge from the security desk.

2. SYSTEM ACCESS AND IT PROVISIONING
Getting you connected is our top priority.
- Hardware: IT will hand over your pre-configured corporate laptop (standard issue is a Lenovo ThinkPad) during the post-lunch IT session on Day 1.
- Initial Login: Follow the instructions provided in your welcome email to log in using your 'cg-initial' generic password, and immediately reset it via the Self-Service portal.
- Access Permissions: Standard applications (Outlook, Teams, Workday, iEvolve) are provisioned immediately. Specific project tools (AWS, Azure, SAP, JIRA) require manager approval. The standard SLA for project-specific system access is 3 business days from the date of request submission.

3. INDUCTION CYCLE (WEEKS 1-2)
Your onboarding journey extends beyond Day 1 to ensure seamless integration.
- Week 1: Focuses on mandatory compliance training (Code of Conduct, Data Privacy, Information Security) on our iEvolve learning platform. These must be completed within the first 5 days.
- Week 2: Role-specific functional induction. You will meet with your reporting manager to define your Key Result Areas (KRAs) for the first 90 days.
- Buddy Program: You will be assigned a 'Buddy'—a peer from your team who will help you navigate the company culture, answer informal queries, and introduce you to key stakeholders over your first 30 days.

4. iEVOLVE TRAINING PORTAL
Continuous learning is a core value at Capgemini.
- The iEvolve platform hosts over 10,000 courses ranging from technical certifications to soft skills.
- As a new joiner, you are expected to complete your 'First 100 Days' learning path, which will be automatically assigned to your profile.
- You can access iEvolve directly from your SSO dashboard using your corporate credentials.

5. BACKGROUND VERIFICATION (BGV) PROCESS
A thorough BGV is a mandatory condition of your employment.
- Our external partner will verify your educational qualifications, previous employment history, and criminal records.
- You must ensure all information provided during recruitment is accurate and complete.
- The BGV process takes approximately 30-45 days. Any discrepancies found may lead to immediate termination of employment, irrespective of performance. Please cooperate with the BGV team if they request any additional documentation.

6. EMPLOYEE ID CARD PROCESS
Your permanent physical ID card serves as your primary access pass and identification.
- Visit the security office situated on the ground floor between 2:00 PM and 4:00 PM on Day 3 to have your photograph taken.
- The permanent ID card will be issued within 5 to 7 working days. Until then, your temporary pass must be worn visibly at all times.
- Lost ID cards must be immediately reported to the security desk and IT helpdesk to deactivate access privileges.
